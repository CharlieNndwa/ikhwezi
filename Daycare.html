About Us:

Ikhwezilokusa LSEN Centre is a haven for children with special learning needs. We embrace a unique approach to education, fostering an immersive learning environment that caters to each child's individual strengths and abilities. By blending innovative technology with expert educators, we empower our students to unlock their full potential and thrive in a supportive and inclusive atmosphere.
