Ikhwezilokusa: Empowering Holistic Well-being for Adults

✦ Established in 1996

Ikhwezilokusa Home provides a safe and caring space for adults facing mental health challenges. We understand that everyone's experience is different, so we don't offer a one-size-fits-all approach.

Instead, our team works closely with residents to create personalized plans that focus on getting better, staying active, and getting the support they need. Our goal is to empower our 72 residents to reach their recovery goals and rejoin their community with confidence.
