Ikhwezilokusa: Psychiatric Recovery for Adults


Established in 2006, Ikhwezilokusa Psychiatric Home provides a safe and caring space for adults facing mental health challenges. We understand that everyone's experience is different, so we don't offer a one-size-fits-all approach.
Instead, our team works closely with residents to create personalized plans that focus on getting better, staying active, and getting the support they need.

We use a combination of skill-building activities, therapy, and care that's tailored to each person's situation. Our goal is to empower residents to reach their recovery goals and feel confident about rejoining their community.

The Home currently cares for 72 adults, creating a supportive community where residents can learn from and uplift each other on their paths to recovery.
